IOSCO Compliance
17 Compliant · 2 Targeted · 3 Not Applicable

Voluntary alignment with all 19 IOSCO Principles for Financial Benchmarks. No other GPU compute benchmark has published an IOSCO compliance statement. Targeted: P5 Oversight Board (due March 2027) and P17 Annual Independent Audit (first full calendar year). Not Applicable: P11, P12, P14 — submitter-related principles; CRI uses automated data collection with no human submitters. Full principle-by-principle assessment in the Governance Framework.
