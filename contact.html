Who Engages CCIR
Built for transaction practitioners.

CCIR's services are designed for professionals working on active GPU-backed credit — not general market participants. The right contact is any party with a role in documenting, underwriting, or monitoring a GPU-collateralized facility.

Lenders
Credit Officers & Underwriters
Structuring GPU-backed term loans, revolvers, or equipment finance facilities where the collateral is H100, H200, or B200 infrastructure. Covenant calibration from underwriting through maturity.
Counsel
Structured Finance Attorneys
Drafting credit documentation that requires a named, independently verifiable reference rate in GPU collateral covenants. CCIR provides the rate definition, covenant language, and Calculation Date packages.
Analysts
Credit Analysts & Rating Staff
Evaluating GPU-collateralized exposure — stress scenarios, collateral coverage analysis, generation obsolescence risk. CRI-H100 and the Generation Spread provide the empirical data anchors that analyst judgment currently lacks.
