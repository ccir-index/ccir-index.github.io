CRI v1.0.0 — Initial Publication
First publication of the CRI index family under Methodology v1.0.0. Four published CRI-8G reference rates — the benchmark rate for 8-GPU InfiniBand node on-demand compute. Full governance stack published alongside rate data.
  • Published rates: CRI-H100, CRI-A100, CRI-H200, CRI-B200 — 8-GPU InfiniBand node on-demand rate per GPU per hour
  • Publication threshold: 4 sources minimum per model. Below 4, rate computed but not published.
  • Sources: RunPod Cluster, Lambda Cluster, Hyperstack, Vultr, Crusoe, Voltage Park, Genesis Cloud, Together AI
  • Surveillance only: Vast.ai (interruptible), Shadeform (aggregator), Thunder (PCIe), Scaleway (PCIe), CoreWeave (single-source enterprise)
  • Computation: Flat median across all eligible observations in trailing 7-day window. Daily source dedup (one observation per source per model per day).
  • Pipeline: Azure Databricks, Delta Lake medallion architecture, config-driven engines, nightly audit
