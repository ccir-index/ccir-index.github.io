Methodology Changelog

Every change to the CRI methodology, index scope, data sources, or governance framework is classified, documented with rationale, and versioned. Historical index values computed under a prior methodology version are never restated. This log is the public record of that discipline.

Initial Publication
CRI v1.0.0 — Initial Publication
First publication of the CRI index family under Methodology v1.0.0. Five spot indices and six cluster indices across two tiers. Full governance stack published alongside rate data.
  • Spot indices: CRI-H100, CRI-A100, CRI-H200, CRI-L40S, CRI-B200
  • Cluster indices (MM): CRI-H100-CLS-MM, CRI-A100-CLS-MM, CRI-B200-CLS-MM
  • Cluster indices (ENT): CRI-H100-CLS-ENT, CRI-H200-CLS-ENT, CRI-B200-CLS-ENT
  • Primary rate: seller_ask_noninterruptible — on-demand spot, no preemption risk
  • Sources: RunPod, Shadeform, Lambda Labs, TensorDock, Nebius (spot); RunPod Cluster, Lambda Cluster (MM); CoreWeave (ENT)
  • Computation: Flat median (percentile_approx 0.5) across all Silver-eligible observations in trailing 7-day window
  • Pipeline: Azure Databricks, Delta Lake medallion architecture, nightly audit_pipeline + weekly audit_sources
